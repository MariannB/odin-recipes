The Ultimate Margarita

Description

A night doesn't start until you have a drink in your hand. Make this margarita and you will be king host amongts your friends.


Ingredients

  • Oranque Liqueur
  • Tequila Silver
  • Fresh Lime juice
  • Sugar
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Shake together Ingredients
  2. Rub the cut side of a lime on the glass rim and dip into salt

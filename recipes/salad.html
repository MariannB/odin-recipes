Spring Salad

Description

People are surprised when they taste this salad with the odd combination of ingredients, but it's a very very good salad.


Ingredients

  • Bacon
  • Celery
  • Lettuce
  • Onions
  • Broccoli

Steps

  1. Place beacon on skillet and cook well
  2. Cut all ingredients and mix
  3. Add salad dressing of your choice and voila!

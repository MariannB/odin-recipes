Lasagna

Description

Zucchini slices step in for pasta in this low-carb and gluten-free beef lasagna that is delicious and satisfying; such a crowd pleaser!


Ingredients

  • Zucchini
  • Pasta
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 350C
  2. Cut zucchini slices
  3. Heat Olive Oil and cook beef
  4. Boil water and cook pasta
  5. Start making layers, and voila!
